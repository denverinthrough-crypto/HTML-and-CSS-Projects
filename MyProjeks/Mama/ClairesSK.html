Puto

Puto - Classic Filipino Rice Cake

Puto is a beloved traditional Filipino dessert and a staple in the kakanin family. This soft, fluffy rice cake is lightly sweet and pillowy textur that melts in your mouth. Often topped with cheese or salted egg, puto strikes the perfect balance of flavor and simplicity.

Cassava Cake

Cassava Cake - Sweet Filipino Delight

Cassava Cake is a beloved traditional Filipino dessert made from grated cassava(yucca root), coconut milk, and sugar. Baked to perfection, it has a soft with a creamy caramalized topping that adds richness and depth of flavor. Cassava Cake is a staple in Filipino celebrations, fiestas, and family gatherings.

Biko

Biko - Filipino Traditional Rice Cake

Biko is a classic Filipino rice cake made from glutionous rice, coconut milk, and brown sugar. Known for sticky, chewy texture and rich caramel flavor, Biko is a well-loved delicacy often served during celebrations, fiestas, and family gatherings. It is typically topped with latik - either sweet coconut caramel or cruncy coconut curds - which adds a deeper coconut aroma and a touch of sweetness. Biko is comfortin, filling, and showcases the Filipino love for simple ingredients transformed into something festive and delicious.
